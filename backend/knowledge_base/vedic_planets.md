# Vedic Planets

The nine grahas of Jyotish Shastra are the celestial agents through which karmic
patterns manifest. Each graha carries a distinct nature, set of significations
(karakas), and a network of friendly and hostile relationships with the others.
Understanding their attributes is foundational to any Vedic chart reading.

## Sun

- Nature: Royal, fiery, masculine, sattvic. The atmakaraka (significator of the soul).
- Karaka significations: Soul, vitality, ego, authority, the father, government,
  bones, the heart, leadership, dharma, and self-confidence.
- Exalted in Aries (10°); debilitated in Libra (10°). Owns Leo.
- Friends: Moon, Mars, Jupiter. Enemies: Venus, Saturn. Neutral: Mercury.

## Moon

- Nature: Cool, watery, feminine, sattvic. The manas-karaka (significator of the mind).
- Karaka significations: Mind, emotions, the mother, nourishment, public, water,
  comfort, intuition, the chest, and memory.
- Exalted in Taurus (3°); debilitated in Scorpio (3°). Owns Cancer.
- Friends: Sun, Mercury. Enemies: none classically. Neutral: Mars, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn.

## Mars

- Nature: Fiery, masculine, tamasic. The bhratru-karaka (significator of siblings)
  and the karaka of courage.
- Karaka significations: Energy, courage, brothers, land, real estate, the
  military, surgery, anger, accidents, and discipline.
- Exalted in Capricorn (28°); debilitated in Cancer (28°). Owns Aries and Scorpio.
- Friends: Sun, Moon, Jupiter. Enemies: Mercury. Neutral: Venus, Saturn.

## Mercury

- Nature: Adaptable, neutral gender, rajasic. The karaka of intellect and
  communication.
- Karaka significations: Intelligence, speech, commerce, mathematics, writing,
  young people, nervous system, and analytical reasoning.
- Exalted in Virgo (15°); debilitated in Pisces (15°). Owns Gemini and Virgo.
- Friends: Sun, Venus. Enemies: Moon. Neutral: Mars, Jupiter, Saturn.

## Jupiter

- Nature: Expansive, masculine, sattvic. The guru-karaka (significator of wisdom,
  teachers, husband for women, and children).
- Karaka significations: Wisdom, dharma, prosperity, children, gurus, religion,
  philosophy, the liver, optimism, and spiritual growth.
- Exalted in Cancer (5°); debilitated in Capricorn (5°). Owns Sagittarius and Pisces.
- Friends: Sun, Moon, Mars. Enemies: Mercury, Venus. Neutral: Saturn.

## Venus

- Nature: Watery, feminine, rajasic. The kalatra-karaka (significator of spouse for men)
  and the karaka of pleasure and beauty.
- Karaka significations: Love, marriage, art, music, comforts, vehicles, jewelry,
  reproductive system, partnerships, and refinement.
- Exalted in Pisces (27°); debilitated in Virgo (27°). Owns Taurus and Libra.
- Friends: Mercury, Saturn. Enemies: Sun, Moon. Neutral: Mars, Jupiter.

## Saturn

- Nature: Slow, dry, neuter, tamasic. The ayur-karaka (significator of longevity)
  and the karaka of discipline and karma.
- Karaka significations: Discipline, work, longevity, restriction, the elderly,
  servants, oil, iron, joints, chronic illness, and detachment.
- Exalted in Libra (20°); debilitated in Aries (20°). Owns Capricorn and Aquarius.
- Friends: Mercury, Venus. Enemies: Sun, Moon, Mars. Neutral: Jupiter.

## Rahu

- Nature: Shadow planet, the north lunar node, tamasic, foreign. Always retrograde.
- Karaka significations: Obsession, foreign lands, illusion, technology, sudden
  change, scandal, snakes, and worldly desires.
- Considered exalted in Taurus (some schools say Gemini); debilitated in Scorpio
  (some say Sagittarius). No sign rulership; behaves like the lord of the sign it occupies.
- Friends: Venus, Saturn. Enemies: Sun, Moon, Mars.

## Ketu

- Nature: Shadow planet, the south lunar node, tamasic, ascetic. Always retrograde.
- Karaka significations: Liberation, moksha, detachment, past-life skills,
  spirituality, headless action, and sudden loss.
- Considered exalted in Scorpio; debilitated in Taurus (mirror of Rahu). No
  sign rulership; behaves like the lord of the sign it occupies.
- Friends: Mars, Venus, Saturn. Enemies: Sun, Moon.
