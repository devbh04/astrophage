# Vedic Astrology Knowledge Base — Part 1: Foundations, Planets & Signs

## 1. FOUNDATIONAL CONCEPTS

### 1.1 Vedic vs Western Astrology
Vedic astrology (Jyotisha) differs from Western astrology in several critical ways:

- **Ayanamsha**: Vedic uses the sidereal zodiac (fixed stars); Western uses the tropical zodiac (seasons). The current difference is approximately 23–24° (Lahiri ayanamsha). A person with a Gemini sun in Western astrology may have a Taurus sun in Vedic.
- **Lahiri Ayanamsha**: The official ayanamsha of the Indian government, used in most Vedic software. Current value ~23°51′ (increases ~50″/year).
- **Ascendant centrality**: In Vedic astrology, the Lagna (Ascendant) is arguably more important than the Sun sign. The entire chart is interpreted through house lords and their placements.
- **Nine planets (Navagrahas)**: Sun (Surya), Moon (Chandra), Mars (Mangal), Mercury (Budha), Jupiter (Guru/Brihaspati), Venus (Shukra), Saturn (Shani), Rahu (North Node), Ketu (South Node). Uranus, Neptune, Pluto are not traditionally used in classical Vedic astrology.
- **Whole-sign houses**: Each house = one full sign. The rising sign becomes the entire 1st house; the next sign becomes the entire 2nd house, etc.
- **Nakshatra system**: The Moon's 27-station path through the sky — central to timing (Dasha) and compatibility.
- **Divisional charts (Vargas)**: D-1 (Rashi/natal), D-9 (Navamsha — marriage/inner self), D-10 (Dashamsha — career), D-2 (Hora — wealth), D-3 (Drekkana — siblings) and others.

### 1.2 Chart Construction Overview
1. Convert birth time to UTC, then to Local Sidereal Time (LST).
2. Apply Lahiri ayanamsha to get sidereal positions.
3. Calculate Ascendant degree using oblique ascension formulas.
4. Place each planet in its sign and house.
5. Determine nakshatra (each = 13°20′ of ecliptic; 27 total).
6. Calculate Vimshottari Dasha from Moon's nakshatra position.

### 1.3 House System
Vedic uses whole-sign houses exclusively in classical interpretation:
- House 1 starts at 0° of the rising sign.
- Each subsequent house = the next zodiac sign in sequence.
- The degree of the Ascendant within House 1 matters for timing but doesn't change house assignment.

## 2. THE NINE PLANETS (NAVAGRAHAS)

### 2.1 SURYA — The Sun ☀️

**Basic Nature**: Malefic (krura/papa graha) but royal and life-giving.  
**Sanskrit Names**: Surya, Ravi, Aditya, Bhaskara, Bhanumaan  
**Gender**: Male  
**Element**: Fire  
**Day**: Sunday (Ravivaar)  
**Direction**: East  
**Color**: Golden/Copper/Orange  
**Gemstone**: Ruby (Manikya)  
**Metal**: Gold  
**Deity**: Lord Vishnu / Shiva / Surya Deva  
**Body Parts**: Heart, spine, right eye (male) / left eye (female), bones, digestive system, vitality  
**Taste**: Pungent/spicy  

**Astronomical**: The Sun moves approximately 1° per day. It takes ~30 days to traverse each sign and one year to complete the zodiac.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Soul (Atma), self, ego, identity, individuality
- Father, authority figures, kings/bosses/government
- Vitality, life force, health, immunity
- Fame, recognition, status, ambition
- Creativity, leadership, confidence
- Consciousness, willpower, courage

**Own Sign**: Leo (Simha)  
**Moolatrikona**: Leo 0°–20°  
**Exaltation**: Aries (Mesha) — highest exaltation at 10° Aries  
**Debilitation**: Libra (Tula) — exact debilitation at 10° Libra  
**Friendly planets**: Moon, Mars, Jupiter  
**Enemy planets**: Venus, Saturn  
**Neutral**: Mercury  

**Sun in 12 Signs**:
- **Aries (Exalted)**: Extremely dynamic, courageous, pioneering, natural leaders. Independent streak. Best Sun placement.
- **Taurus**: Determined, value-driven, love of beauty and material security. Slower but persistent.
- **Gemini**: Intellectual, communicative, versatile. May scatter energy; curious and witty leaders.
- **Cancer**: Emotional, nurturing but conflicted between self-assertion and family duty. Intuitive.
- **Leo (Own sign)**: Natural royalty, charismatic, generous, proud, theatrical. Strong identity.
- **Virgo**: Analytical, detail-oriented, service-minded. Can be overly critical of self and others.
- **Libra (Debilitated)**: Identity conflicts, indecisive, may struggle with self-assertion. Diplomatic but weak-willed. Exact debilitation at 10°.
- **Scorpio**: Intense, investigative, secretive, powerful will, transformative experiences.
- **Sagittarius**: Philosophical, optimistic, teaching, travel, higher wisdom. Generous spirit.
- **Capricorn**: Disciplined, ambitious, career-focused. Father may be strict or distant.
- **Aquarius**: Humanitarian, unconventional, intellectual. Leadership via community service.
- **Pisces**: Spiritual, compassionate, imaginative. May struggle with boundaries and clarity.

**Sun in 12 Houses**:
- **1st**: Strong physical constitution, natural authority, prominent personality. Can be domineering. Good for leadership.
- **2nd**: Wealth through government/father, strong voice, family pride. May be authoritative in speech.
- **3rd**: Courage, self-promotion, skilled communicator, sibling competition.
- **4th**: Father may be absent or domineering. Real estate gains. Inner conflict about home vs. career.
- **5th**: Creative intelligence, children (especially first child), speculation, education, romance.
- **6th**: Service to authority, health consciousness, overcoming enemies. May work in government/medical.
- **7th**: Powerful spouse, business partnerships, may overshadow partners. Marriage to authority figure.
- **8th**: Hidden power, inheritance, research, occult. Father may face difficulties. Longevity of father variable.
- **9th**: Very auspicious — dharmic, philosophical father, luck through religion, higher education. Natural teacher.
- **10th**: Career in government/leadership, high status, father connection to career. Raj Yoga if well aspected.
- **11th**: Gains through government, prominent social circle, older siblings may be influential.
- **12th**: Foreign residence, spirituality, hidden identity, expenses. Father may be in foreign land or spiritual.

### 2.2 CHANDRA — The Moon 🌙

**Basic Nature**: Benefic (when waxing/Shukla Paksha); mild malefic (when waning/Krishna Paksha)  
**Sanskrit Names**: Chandra, Soma, Indu, Nisha, Chandrama  
**Gender**: Female  
**Element**: Water  
**Day**: Monday (Somvaar)  
**Direction**: Northwest  
**Color**: White, Silver, Pearl  
**Gemstone**: Pearl (Moti) or Moonstone  
**Metal**: Silver  
**Deity**: Lord Shiva, Parvati  
**Body Parts**: Mind (manas), brain, left eye (male), blood, lymph, stomach, breasts, uterus, emotions  
**Taste**: Salty  

**Astronomical**: Fastest moving planet — completes the zodiac in ~27.3 days. Changes signs every ~2.25 days. 

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Mind, emotions, feelings, mental health, mood
- Mother, motherhood, nurturing, home
- Subconscious, instincts, habits, memory
- The public, masses, popularity
- Liquids, water, travel by water
- Agriculture, vegetation, milk, food
- Cycles, rhythms, tides

**Own Sign**: Cancer (Karka)  
**Moolatrikona**: Taurus 4°–20°  
**Exaltation**: Taurus (Vrishabha) — highest at 3° Taurus  
**Debilitation**: Scorpio (Vrischika) — exact at 3° Scorpio  
**Friendly planets**: Sun, Mercury  
**Enemy planets**: None (classically)  
**Neutral**: Mars, Jupiter, Venus, Saturn  

**Moon strength rules**:
- Strong when: waxing (Shukla Paksha), in own sign/exaltation, in kendras (1,4,7,10), with benefics
- Weak when: waning (Krishna Paksha), especially within 72° of Sun (combust/asta), in 8th house, afflicted by Rahu/Ketu or Saturn/Mars

**Moon in 12 Signs**:
- **Aries**: Quick emotional reactions, independent, impulsive, courageous, passionate, impatient.
- **Taurus (Exalted)**: Stable emotions, comfort-seeking, sensuous, loyal, steady mind, artistic.
- **Gemini**: Intellectually restless, communicative, curious, moody, loves variety and information.
- **Cancer (Own)**: Highly intuitive, deeply emotional, nurturing, attached to home and past, psychic.
- **Leo**: Proud emotions, dramatic, generous, needs validation, warm, loves attention.
- **Virgo**: Analytical emotions, critical, service-oriented, health-conscious, nervous tendencies.
- **Libra**: Harmonious, relationship-oriented, diplomatic, indecisive, needs balance and beauty.
- **Scorpio (Debilitated)**: Intense, secretive, transformative emotions, deep psychology, possible emotional turbulence.
- **Sagittarius**: Optimistic, philosophical, freedom-loving, emotionally expansive, truth-seeking.
- **Capricorn**: Reserved emotions, disciplined, ambitious, may feel lonely, emotionally self-sufficient.
- **Aquarius**: Detached emotions, humanitarian, unconventional, intellectual, needs freedom.
- **Pisces**: Compassionate, dreamy, psychic, emotional depth, artistic, possibly escapist.

### 2.3 MANGAL — Mars ♂️

**Basic Nature**: Malefic (natural), first-rate cruel planet  
**Sanskrit Names**: Mangal, Kuja, Angaraka, Bhauma, Chevvai (Tamil)  
**Gender**: Male  
**Element**: Fire  
**Day**: Tuesday (Mangalvaar)  
**Direction**: South  
**Color**: Red, Blood red  
**Gemstone**: Red Coral (Moonga)  
**Metal**: Copper, Iron  
**Deity**: Lord Kartikeya (Murugan), Lord Hanuman  
**Body Parts**: Blood, muscles, energy, bone marrow, head, nose, forehead, bile, male reproductive system  
**Taste**: Bitter  

**Astronomical**: Mars takes ~2 years to orbit the Sun. Stays in each sign ~45 days (can extend to 6-7 months when retrograde).

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Courage, valor, aggression, action, ambition
- Brothers (especially younger), male friends
- Sports, military, police, surgeons, engineers
- Land, property, real estate
- Weapons, accidents, fire, violence
- Physical strength, stamina, competitive drive
- Passion, sexuality (male), desire

**Own Signs**: Aries (Mesha) and Scorpio (Vrischika)  
**Moolatrikona**: Aries 0°–12°  
**Exaltation**: Capricorn (Makara) — highest at 28° Capricorn  
**Debilitation**: Cancer (Karka) — exact at 28° Cancer  
**Friendly planets**: Sun, Moon, Jupiter  
**Enemy planets**: Mercury, Saturn  
**Neutral**: Venus  

**Mangal Dosha (Kuja Dosha)**:
Mars in houses 1, 2, 4, 7, 8, or 12 from Lagna, Moon, or Venus creates Mangal Dosha.
- Traditionally believed to cause marital discord, delay in marriage, or harm to spouse.
- **Most potent positions**: 7th and 8th house.
- **Cancellations (Dosha parihara)**: Both partners have Mangal Dosha (cancels each other); Mars in own/exalted sign; Mars in 2nd house but aspected by Jupiter; specific sign placements cancel dosha (Mars in Aries in 2nd, Mars in Scorpio in 8th, etc.).
- **Modern view**: Mangal Dosha is most significant when Mars afflicts the 7th house or 7th lord directly.

### 2.4 BUDHA — Mercury ☿

**Basic Nature**: Benefic with benefics, malefic with malefics (dual nature)  
**Sanskrit Names**: Budha, Soumya, Bodhana  
**Gender**: Neuter/Neutral  
**Element**: Earth  
**Day**: Wednesday (Budhvaar)  
**Direction**: North  
**Color**: Green, Emerald green  
**Gemstone**: Emerald (Panna)  
**Metal**: Brass, Bronze  
**Deity**: Lord Vishnu, Saraswati  
**Body Parts**: Nervous system, skin, hands, arms, lungs, intestines, speech organs, memory  
**Taste**: Mixed (all six tastes)  

**Astronomical**: Mercury never goes more than 28° from the Sun. Stays in each sign ~14–30 days. Goes retrograde ~3 times a year for 3 weeks each.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Intelligence, intellect, logic, reasoning
- Communication, speech, writing, journalism
- Trade, commerce, business, accounting
- Education, learning, analysis, discrimination
- Adaptability, wit, humor, cleverness
- Siblings, cousins, neighbors
- Mathematics, astrology, computing

**Own Signs**: Gemini (Mithuna) and Virgo (Kanya)  
**Moolatrikona**: Virgo 16°–20°  
**Exaltation**: Virgo (Kanya) — highest at 15° Virgo  
**Debilitation**: Pisces (Meena) — exact at 15° Pisces  
**Friendly planets**: Sun, Venus  
**Enemy planets**: Moon  
**Neutral**: Mars, Jupiter, Saturn  

**Combust (Asta)**: Mercury within 14° of Sun (12° for retrograde Mercury) loses strength. However, Mercury is naturally close to the Sun, so this is common.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:

**Friendly planets**: Sun, Moon, Mars

**Jupiter in Key Positions**:

### 2.7 SHANI — Saturn ♄

**Basic Nature**: Strongest malefic (though it rewards the patient and righteous)  
**Sanskrit Names**: Shani, Shanaiswara, Manda, Sour, Yama's brother  
**Gender**: Neutral/Male  
**Element**: Air  
**Day**: Saturday (Shanivaar)  
**Direction**: West  
**Color**: Dark blue, Black, Purple  
**Gemstone**: Blue Sapphire (Neelam) — most powerful and potentially dangerous gem  
**Metal**: Iron, Lead  
**Deity**: Bhairav, Yama, Hanuman (protects from Saturn's ill effects)  
**Body Parts**: Bones, teeth, joints, knees, spine, skin (dryness), neurological system, chronic ailments  
**Taste**: Astringent  

**Astronomical**: Saturn takes ~29.5 years to orbit the Sun (~2.5 years per sign). Retrograde ~4.5 months each year.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Karma, discipline, responsibility, hard work, delays
- Old age, longevity, chronic illness, death (secondary)
- Servants, laborers, masses, the poor
- Restrictions, limitations, suffering, lessons
- Detachment, asceticism, meditation, moksha
- Oil, coal, iron, land, agriculture (with Mars)
- Law, administration, bureaucracy
- Longevity (ayushkaraka)

**Own Signs**: Capricorn (Makara) and Aquarius (Kumbha)  
**Moolatrikona**: Aquarius 0°–20°  
**Exaltation**: Libra (Tula) — highest at 20° Libra  
**Debilitation**: Aries (Mesha) — exact at 20° Aries  
**Friendly planets**: Mercury, Venus  
**Enemy planets**: Sun, Moon, Mars  
**Neutral**: Jupiter  

**Sade Sati**: Saturn's 7.5-year transit over natal Moon (the sign before, the natal Moon sign, and the sign after). Considered highly challenging.

**Shani Dhaiya (Kantaka Shani)**: Saturn transiting the 4th or 8th house from natal Moon. Lasts 2.5 years each.

### 2.8 RAHU — North Lunar Node ☊

**Basic Nature**: Shadow planet (Chhaya graha), extreme malefic  
**Sanskrit Names**: Rahu, Swarbhanu, Asura, Dragon's Head  
**Gender**: Neutral (acts as male in interpretation)  
**Element**: Air (some say all elements — chameleon)  
**Day**: Saturday (shares with Saturn) / some say Wednesday  
**Direction**: Southwest  
**Color**: Smoky grey, Black, Ultraviolet  
**Gemstone**: Hessonite (Gomed/Gomedha)  
**Metal**: Lead, Mixed metals  
**Deity**: Durga (some traditions), no traditional deity — a demon  
**Body Parts**: Nervous system, skin diseases, epidemics, foreign matter in body  

**Nature**: Rahu has no physical body — it is a mathematical point (where Moon's path crosses the ecliptic going northward). Behaves like Saturn in some ways, like a super-Venus in others. Amplifies and distorts whatever it touches.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Foreign things, foreign travel, foreign residence
- Technology, computers, unconventional paths
- Obsession, illusion, maya, confusion
- Politics, manipulation, mass influence
- Sudden events, breaks from tradition
- Paternal grandparents, outcasts
- Poisons, drugs, alcohol, epidemics
- Past-life desires brought into this life

**Own Sign**: Gemini (or Virgo by some traditions — disputed)  
**Exaltation**: Taurus (Gemini by some authorities)  
**Debilitation**: Scorpio (Sagittarius by some)  
**Rahu always moves retrograde** in true node calculation.  

**Rahu conjunct or aspecting**: Sun → ego/identity confusion, issues with father/authority; Moon → emotional turbulence, mother issues, mental health; Jupiter → questioning religion/tradition, unconventional wisdom; Saturn → amplified karmic lessons, paranoia.

**Rahu's Transit**: Takes ~18 months per sign. Currently (2025-2026): Pisces.

### 2.9 KETU — South Lunar Node ☋

**Basic Nature**: Shadow planet, moksha karaka, spiritual liberation  
**Sanskrit Names**: Ketu, Dragon's Tail, Shikhi  
**Gender**: Neutral (acts as female, spiritual)  
**Element**: Fire  
**Day**: Tuesday (some say shares Saturn's day)  
**Direction**: Northwest  
**Color**: Multicolored, variegated, smoky  
**Gemstone**: Cat's Eye (Lahsuniya/Vaidurya)  
**Metal**: Mixed/Iron  
**Deity**: Ganesha, Chitragupta, Bhairav  
**Body Parts**: Lower extremities, mysterious diseases, moksha through dissolution  

**Nature**: Always opposite Rahu (they are always 180° apart). Ketu represents past-life mastery, spirituality, liberation, detachment, and loss. Where Rahu obsesses and grasps, Ketu detaches and dissolves.

**Significations (Karakatvas)**:
- Moksha, spirituality, liberation, enlightenment
- Past-life karma already mastered (hence detachment)
- Intuition, psychic abilities, mysticism
- Asceticism, renunciation, isolation
- Maternal grandparents
- Sudden losses that lead to spiritual growth
- Research, investigation, going deep
- Accidents, surgeries (especially mysterious ailments)

**Ketu's Transit**: Exactly opposite Rahu — currently (2025-2026): Virgo.

**Ketu conjunct**: Sun → identity loss/spiritual search; Moon → psychic sensitivity, mother separation; Jupiter → spiritual teacher, past-life religious connection; Venus → past-life romantic karma, artistic gifts, current detachment from relationships.

### 3.2 MESHA — Aries (March 14 – April 14 approx. sidereal)

**Symbol**: Ram  
**Element**: Fire  
**Mode**: Cardinal (Chara)  
**Ruler**: Mars  
**Direction**: East  
**Gender**: Male  
**Color**: Red  
**Lucky Numbers**: 1, 8, 17  
**Body Part**: Head, face, brain  

**Core Nature**: The first sign, Aries embodies pure raw energy, initiation, courage, and the drive to begin. It is the self in its most primal, unrefined form — passionate, direct, and impulsive. Aries natives are natural pioneers, first movers, and natural athletes or warriors.

**Positive Traits**: Courageous, enthusiastic, direct, honest, pioneering, independent, athletic, passionate  
**Challenging Traits**: Impulsive, aggressive, impatient, self-centered, hot-headed, careless, argumentative  

**In the Lagna (Ascendant)**: Mars-ruled — physical body tends to be strong, athletic, medium height. Personality is bold and direct. May have scar on face/head. Active, competitive, may struggle with anger. The native often has a sharp or prominent nose.

**Planets in Aries**:
- Sun (Exalted at 10°): Maximum vitality, leadership, confidence
- Moon: Quick reactions, impulsive feelings, passionate
- Mars (Own sign): Full strength — warrior energy, courage
- Mercury: Sharp and direct thinking, fast but sometimes reckless
- Jupiter: Expanding courage, philosophical independence
- Venus: Love of excitement but impulsive in relationships
- Saturn (Debilitated at 20°): Weakest — struggles with restrictions, impatient

## 5. RETROGRADE PLANETS (VAKRI GRAHA)

A planet appears retrograde (vakri) when Earth overtakes it in orbital speed, creating an optical backward motion. The Sun and Moon never retrograde. Rahu and Ketu are always (mean node) or frequently (true node) retrograde.

**Effects of Retrograde**:
- The planet's energy turns inward — more intense, internalized, introspective
- Re-doing, re-visiting, re-examining themes of that planet
- The planet is actually closest to Earth during retrograde → stronger in effects (though quality may be mixed)
- Retrograde planets in natal chart → past-life themes, karma being brought forward

**Retrograde in Transit**:
- Mercury retrograde: Communication breakdowns, technical issues, travel delays (3×/year for ~21 days)
- Venus retrograde: Relationship review, reconnecting with ex, reassessing values (1×/18 months for ~40 days)
- Mars retrograde: Delayed actions, redirected energy, reassessing ambitions (1×/2 years for ~70 days)
- Jupiter retrograde: Inner philosophical work, less outward expansion (1×/year for ~120 days)
- Saturn retrograde: Reviewing karmic lessons, delayed responsibilities (1×/year for ~140 days)
